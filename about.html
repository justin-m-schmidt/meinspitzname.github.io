The BrincoPro Story

After my four grandkids were fascinating by jumping in our bed, they will always end up breaking it. That’s when we came up with the idea, why not creating a commodity specialized for them to jump on? That was the moment when Brinco pro was born, at first it was hard creating a model from scratch with the objective to have an elastic platform to jump on. My wife and me didn’t gave up because we wanted to give this fun activity for our grandkids to have when they came to visit us.


After years of work, we succeed creating this entertainment which not only kids and adolescents love but it's also a way to exercise while having fun. From all these experiences, we have found our 3 centers of excellence:
